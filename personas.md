# Personas

## Persona 1: Overwhelmed College Student

Name: Maya  
Context: Maya is taking four classes and working part-time.  
Goal: Figure out what to do first when several deadlines are close together.  
Pain Points:
- Does not know which assignment matters most
- Underestimates how long work will take
- Wants reassurance even when the plan is unrealistic
- Feels stuck when everything seems urgent

## Persona 2: Working Student

Name: Jordan  
Context: Jordan works during the day and studies at night.  
Goal: Create a realistic evening plan that accounts for limited energy.  
Pain Points:
- Cannot study for long blocks after work
- Needs short next actions
- Often has less usable time than expected

## Persona 3: Academic Support Coach

Name: Taylor  
Context: Taylor helps students recover when they fall behind.  
Goal: Quickly identify risk, missing information, and next steps.  
Pain Points:
- Student information is often messy
- Plans need to be realistic
- Students may wait too long to contact instructors
